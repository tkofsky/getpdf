§2.32(a)(6); TMEP §1402.01.  Applicant must amend the identification to specify the common 
commercial or generic name of the goods/services.  See TMEP §1402.01.  If the goods have no 
common commercial or generic name, applicant must describe the product, its main purpose, and its 
intended uses.  See id.  If the services have no common commercial or generic name, applicant must 
describe or explain the nature of the services using clear and succinct language.  See id.
